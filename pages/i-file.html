I-FILE MO: Integrated Complaint Management System.

About.

Monitoring of Case Records submitted by other departments. The system allows users to track the status of cases, manage documents, and communicate with relevant parties. It aims to streamline the complaint process and improve efficiency in handling cases.

Company:

National Labor Relations Commission Regional Arbitration Branch I (NLRC).

Location:

San Fernando City, La Union, Philippines

Date:

August 2024 - January 2025

Challenges.

Some challenges I faced during the development of the system include ensuring data security, integrating with existing databases, and providing a user-friendly interface. Another major hurdle was handling the full process of filed documents from the moment they're submitted to the point they're finalized. This required carefully aligning the interface with the underlying database logic to make sure every step of the workflow was accurately tracked and stored.

Results.

The system successfully streamlined the document filing process, reducing manual errors and improving overall efficiency. Executive Arbiter found the interface intuitive and easy to navigate, and the integration with the database ensured that data remained consistent and secure throughout each stage. As a result, the workflow became more transparent and traceable, allowing staff to handle documents more effectively from submission to completion.
